Frozen Chocolate Banana

  • Time: 90
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Banana 4 medium ripe
Chocolate 16 ounces
Popsicle Sticks 4 wooden

Step 1: Peel the four bananas.

Step 2: Melt chocolate on low heat in medium sauce pan.

Step 3: Put popsicle stick inside banana, leaving enough out to make a handle.

Step 4: Dip banana in chocolate. Coat completely.

Step 5: Place chocolate coated banana on a cookie sheet.

Step 6: Place in freeze for one hour or until frozen.

Cheese Cake No Bake

  • Time: 6 1/2 hours
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Cream Cheese 2 16oz packages
White Sugar 1 cup
Graham Cracker 1 pie crust
Cherry Pie Filling 16 ounce

Step 1: Soften Cream Cheese by leaving on counter for 3 hours.

Step 2: Mix Cream Cheese and White Sugar in a bowl until a smooth consistency.

Step 3: Place mixture in Graham Cracker Pie.

Step 4: Pour Cherry Pie Filling on top of mixture.

Step 5: Put Pie in the refrigerator to let set for 3 hours.

Step 6: Take Pie out, cut into 8 pieces, and eat!

Apple Crisp

  • Time: 1 1/2 hours
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
Apples 10
Brown Sugar 1 Cup
Oatmeal 3 Cups
Butter 8 Ounces

Step 1: Soften butter by microwaving 1 minute or until soft.

Step 2: Mix Brown Sugar, Oatmeal, Butter in a bowl until crumbly. Set aside.

Step 3: Wash and cut apples into 8 slices.

Step 4: Place apples into a greased 8 x 10 baking pan.

Step 5: Evenly sprinkle the mixture you made on top of the apple slices.

Step 6: Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 1 hour

Step 7: Take out of oven. Let cool for 10 minutes. Cut into 6 even portions.
